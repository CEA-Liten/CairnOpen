Show/Hide MultiObjective components

The user can show/hide MultiObjective components (and their links) by clicking the dedicated button on the Toolbar as shown in Fig. 42 and Fig. 43.



The MultiObjective components are shown when the color of the button is green, and they are hidden when the color of the button is red.

Note

Hiding the MultiObjective components doesn’t modify the study and doesn’t affect the result of the simulation. It is only a feature to display the architecture without the MultiObjective connections.
