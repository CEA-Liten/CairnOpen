How to link components?

After placing the components, connect them together by defining the carrier that is used by each component. To do so, right click on the arrows and select Set Carrier.

These connections will form the energy balance and set the groundwork for defining the system’s constraints.

As you continue, ensure that the setup of the components mirrors the conceptual system you’ve imagined.

In order to connect the components between eachother and ensure the energy balance, a Node law or a ** NodeEquality** is necessary to perform flow balances ; technological components cannot be connected directly but through buses.

Note

Three types of buses exist:

  • Node law

  • NodeEquality

  • MutliObjective

Note

The energy vector propagates when connecting one port to another: the energy vector of the arrival port automatically aligns with that of the departure port.
